    CAPTION FORMULA:¶

    Every image should include:

    1. Image itself with clear filename
    2. Disclaimer line making clear it's reconstruction:
    3. "Artistic reconstruction..."
    4. "Speculative visualization..."
    5. "Historical recreation based on..."
    6. Context explaining what the entry contains
    7. Optional: Visual details to notice ("Note the tear stains..." "Heavy ink suggests...")

    LEGAL SAFETY REMINDER:¶

    ALWAYS include disclaimer that these are: - ✅ Historical reconstructions - ✅ Artistic visualizations - ✅ Speculative recreations - ✅ Based on research, not actual discovered documents

    NEVER claim: - ❌ These are photographs of real manuscript - ❌ These were "discovered" or "authenticated" - ❌ These prove the story definitively

    The images serve to ILLUSTRATE the narrative, not FABRICATE evidence.

    The power is in the QUESTIONS they raise, not false claims of proof.
