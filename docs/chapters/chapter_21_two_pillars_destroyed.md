## What is Guru Parampara?

### Definition from Hindu Tradition

**Parampara (परम्परा):** ([Wikipedia: Guru-Shishya Tradition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guru%E2%80%93shishya_tradition))

> "An uninterrupted succession of teachers and disciples in traditional Vedic culture. Knowledge is passed down through successive generations. A particular guru lineage is called **parampara**."

**Sampradaya (सम्प्रदाय):** ([Wikipedia: Sampradaya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sampradaya))

> "A living tradition of teaching and practice within a specific religious-spiritual tradition. Composed of a monastic order within a specific guru lineage."

**Key Principle**: One cannot become a member by birth. **You must receive diksha (initiation) from a living guru** who himself was initiated in an unbroken chain going back to the founder.
