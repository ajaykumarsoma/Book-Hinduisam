**Academic Source:**  
> "The neuter noun *gudą* was used in pre-Christian times to refer to an **'idol'** or a **'libated object of worship.'**"

**Oxford English Dictionary:**  
> "The word 'God' is of **Germanic origin**, from *gudą*, originally a **pagan term** for a deity invoked during sacrificial rites."
