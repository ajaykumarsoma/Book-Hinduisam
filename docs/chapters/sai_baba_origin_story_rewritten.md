**Who Were the Pindaris?**

According to the *1911 Encyclopædia Britannica*:

> "The majority of them seem to have been **Mahommedans** [Muslims]... Their special characteristic was that they **received no pay, but rather purchased the privilege of plundering on their own account**."

**Source:** [1911 Encyclopædia Britannica - Pindaris](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/1911_Encyclop%C3%A6dia_Britannica/Pindaris)

### **THIS WAS ISLAMIC JIHAD AGAINST HINDUS**

**Why Target Hindus Specifically?**

1. **Religious Motivation**: Pindaris were predominantly Muslim and saw Hindu villages as **kuffar** (infidel) territory legitimate for **ghazwa** (raids for Islam)

2. **Wealth Concentration**: Hindu merchant communities, zamindars, and temples held most wealth in rural India

3. **Conversion Agenda**: Captured Hindus were forcibly converted or enslaved, expanding dar-al-Islam (house of Islam)

4. **Cultural Destruction**: Temples were prime targets for plunder (gold ornaments, jewels) and desecration
