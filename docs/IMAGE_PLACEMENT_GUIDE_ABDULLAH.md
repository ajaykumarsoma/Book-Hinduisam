## CAPTION FORMULA:

Every image should include:

1. **Image itself** with clear filename
2. **Disclaimer line** making clear it's reconstruction:
   - "Artistic reconstruction..."
   - "Speculative visualization..."
   - "Historical recreation based on..."
3. **Context** explaining what the entry contains
4. **Optional:** Visual details to notice ("Note the tear stains..." "Heavy ink suggests...")

---

## LEGAL SAFETY REMINDER:

**ALWAYS include disclaimer that these are:**
- ✅ Historical reconstructions
- ✅ Artistic visualizations
- ✅ Speculative recreations
- ✅ Based on research, not actual discovered documents

**NEVER claim:**
- ❌ These are photographs of real manuscript
- ❌ These were "discovered" or "authenticated"
- ❌ These prove the story definitively

---

**The images serve to ILLUSTRATE the narrative, not FABRICATE evidence.**

**The power is in the QUESTIONS they raise, not false claims of proof.**
