    Bibliography¶

    Primary sources and scholarly works referenced in this book.

    Primary Sanskrit Texts¶

    Vedas¶

    • Ṛg Veda — Oldest Vedic text (c. 1500-1200 BCE); hymns to various deities
    • Yajur Veda — Ritual procedures and sacrificial formulas
    • Sāma Veda — Musical chants derived from Ṛg Veda
    • Atharva Veda — Spells, charms, and philosophical hymns

    Upaniṣads¶

    • Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad — One of the oldest; focus on Ātman-Brahman identity
    • Chāndogya Upaniṣad — Teachings on meditation and the nature of reality
    • Kaṭha Upaniṣad — Dialogue between Naciketas and Yama on death and Ātman
    • Īśa Upaniṣad — Brief text on the divine pervading all existence

    Bhagavad Gītā¶

    • Part of the Mahābhārata; dialogue between Kṛṣṇa and Arjuna on duty and yoga

    Āgamas¶

    • Vaikhānasa Āgama — Vaiṣṇava temple worship
    • Pāñcarātra Saṃhitās — Vaiṣṇava ritual manuals
    • Kāmika Āgama — Śaiva temple procedures
    • Tantra Śāstras — Śākta worship and practices

    Śilpa & Vāstu Texts¶

    • Mayamata — Tamil architectural treatise
    • Mānasāra Śilpa Śāstra — Iconography and proportion
    • Bṛhat Saṃhitā (Varāhamihira) — Chapter 60 on murti installation
